# MRE Timeline

## In-Order Timeline (Approx)
1. Research in summer
2. Fall semester starts
3. Begin project (September-October)
4. Finish project (late October)
5. Start writing (late October)
6. Ch 1 = title pg + space for abstract + acknowledgements list + ToC + LoF + LoA + intro
7. Ch 2 = methods
8. Ch 3 = process
9. Ch 4 = conclusion + bibliography + glossary + appendices; approx 40-60 pgs total (done by end of January)
10. edit and professionalize (February 18 at latest)
11. hand in to send to reviewers (March 18, latest March 25)
12. defend April 15th ish (check what the specific due date is)

## Parts of an MRE (based on CW's):
1. Title page
2. Abstract
3. Acknowledgements
4. Table of Contents
5. List of Figures
6. List of Appendices
7. Introduction (7 pgs) + intro to sources and methods chosen
8. Methods (9 pgs) + why method + history of method
9. Process (17 pgs) + how you did it + why you did it
10. Conclusion (3 pgs)
11. Bibliography
12. Glossary
13. Appendix A, B, etc.
